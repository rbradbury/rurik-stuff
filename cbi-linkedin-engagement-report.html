What the data says

Key insights

4×
Impression growth, S1 → S3
The final 50 days produced 39K impressions vs. 10K in the first — nearly four times the reach at the same posting cadence.
50×
Reaction growth, S1 → S3
Reactions jumped from 8 total in S1 to 403 in S3. Reactions require deliberate intent — the clearest signal of content resonance.
—
Click rate in S3
Nearly double S1's 2.8%. Higher volume didn't come at the expense of quality — audiences clicked more, not less, as reach grew.
May 6
AI 100 post outperformed all of S1
The May 6 spike (4,760 impressions, 450 clicks) exceeded S1's entire 50-day total on its own.
—
Comments + reposts in S3
Near-zero in S1 and S2. Reposts signal someone put their own name behind the content — a meaningful quality shift.
S2
The bridge period
Mar–Apr saw reactions emerge for the first time and impressions grow modestly — the setup for S3's acceleration, not a plateau.
